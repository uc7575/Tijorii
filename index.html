Our Story

Born from a Belief That India Deserves Better

For years, buying a premium suitcase in India meant one thing: paying an Indian price for a Chinese product. Every major luggage brand you'd see at airports — the ones with glossy packaging and confident "Made in India" marketing — were quietly assembling their products in factories in Guangzhou and Shenzhen. The shell, the wheels, the locks, the lining: all sourced from the same suppliers that make a hundred other forgettable bags. We found that unacceptable.

Tijorii began with a single question: what would an Indian suitcase look like if we actually made it in India — not as a cost-saving compromise, but as a statement of craft? The founder spent two years visiting workshops in Pune's metalworking districts, meeting aluminium fabricators who had supplied parts for aerospace and defence projects. What they could do with a sheet of 3003-series aluminium was extraordinary. The shells came alive.

The interior was the harder problem. Polyester was not an option. A chance visit to a leatherworking cooperative in Jaipur led to an unexpected discovery: families of artisans who had spent generations crafting embroidered leather goods for royal households — their skills entirely intact, their livelihoods precarious. The block-print workshops of Sanganer. The silk looms of Varanasi. The vegetable-tannery lanes of Kanpur. India had everything. It had always had everything.

Tijorii is the meeting point of aerospace-grade precision and centuries-old Indian craftsmanship. Every suitcase is a collaboration across three cities. Every interior is stitched by hand. No two are identical. That is not a limitation — that is the point.

Craftsmanship & Build Quality

Every Detail, Considered

From the aluminum shell to the final stitch — nothing is outsourced, nothing is rushed.

Aerospace-Grade Aluminum Shell

Our shells are precision-pressed from 3003-series aluminum alloy — the same grade used in aerospace panels. Lightweight yet extraordinarily strong, each shell is brushed and anodized for a satin finish that resists scratches, dents, and the chaos of modern travel.

Japanese TSA-Approved Lock System

Each Tijorii is fitted with a dual-frame aluminum locking system paired with a Japanese TSA-approved combination lock. The frame closes with a satisfying click — flush, secure, and tamper-resistant.

Silent Japanese Spinner Wheels

Our 360° multi-directional spinner wheels use Japanese-engineered bearings for near-silent glide on any surface. The wheel housing is aluminum-reinforced, not plastic.

Hand-Embroidered Silk Interior Lining

The interior is lined with Banarasi silk, hand-embroidered by artisans in Varanasi. Each panel features a unique block print motif — no two Tijoriis are identical.

Full-Grain Leather Corner Detailing

Full-grain vegetable-tanned leather sourced from Kanpur's tanneries reinforces the exterior corners and trim — adding both protection and an unmistakably Indian luxury character.

Hand-Stitched Suede Dividers & Pockets

Interior pockets and compression dividers are crafted from Rajasthani suede — hand-stitched, hand-finished, and designed to cradle your belongings with the same care as a bespoke garment.
